      Thor Ragnarok

      Thor Ragnarok is an amazing movie. It is so much better than the other Thor movies and makes it more fun. Ragnarok is a Hilarious movie and makes me so happy. The vibe of Thor Ragnarok is just so amazing, happy, funny and great. This is everything you want out of Thor and Loki. It finishes out the story arc for Loki showing that he can be a hero and introduces the story of the Valkyrie. Ragnarok also sets up the next Avengers movie, Infinity war. This movie's plott is less serious than any Thor movie. The addition of Bruce Banner in this Thor movie is a duo that we have never seen before but totally works and sets his story arc up for Infinity war. The director Taika Waititi is so amazing and does a great job. Not only is a great director but he also plays Korg in the movie. Korg is the comedic relief and such a great character. Taika Waititi is so funny and good, he brings a new version of Thor and I am so excited to see what else he does in the MCU.

      Guardians of the Galaxy

      Guardians of the Galaxy is a fun, energetic movie with great music. The vibe is very similar to Thor Ragnarok, comedic but still has a great plot. The Guardians of the Galaxy are a group of people who meet in an intergalactic prison and how they become a family while saving the world from a power hungry crazy person with an infinity stone. The story mainly follows peter Quill right after his mother dies and then he was abducted by a group of alien Ravagers. He was raised with them and spends his adult life as a scraper getting jobs around the galaxy.
